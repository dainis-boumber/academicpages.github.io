Help

  • Click just before the phrase below the instructions to hear the entire sentence spoken correctly.
  • Click on any word to hear it pronounced correctly.
  • Push "Record"-button and read aloud the phrase displayed on the screen below these instructions.
  • Your voice will be recognized by Google Speech API, and you will see the mispronounced phrases, if there are any.
  • You will see the sentence change color, green for correctly spoken parts, red for incorrectly ones.
  • Click under it to cycle to what the A.I. thought you may have said along with the confidence score.
  • Listen to your voice by clicking on the "Listen" button to detect hear the defects in pronunciation.
